About Nobel Civils

Background

Who We Are

Nobel Civils is a 100% Namibian contractor registered in 2016 as an MSME. The company specializes in rail, civil and building works, delivering quality, efficient and affordable construction services.

Over the years, Nobel Civils has acquired the skills, resources and partnerships needed to execute sizeable projects to client satisfaction, combining in-house capability with a strong network of outsourcing partners.

Mission & Strategy

Customer-Focused Construction Solutions

Our Mission

To provide quality, efficient and affordable services while delivering sustainable projects with long-term social and environmental impact.

Customer Focus

To execute projects according to client requirements while balancing quality, time and budget.

Our Strategy

To use lean, customer-centric and cost-effective construction methods supported by research, evidence-based metrics and ethical business practice.
