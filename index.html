Background

Nobel Civils

Nobel Civils is a 100% bona-fide Namibian contractor registered as an MSME with the Ministry of Trade. The company is black-owned and managed, specializing in rail, civil and building works.

The company combines quality delivery with cost-saving methodology through skilled internal resources and a strong network of outsourcing partners.
