Reliable Delivery Across Key Infrastructure Projects

Nobel Civils has participated in railway level crossing, municipal services, road-support, civil and building-related projects across Namibia.

2026 • Walvis Bay

Expansion of Level Crossing on Railway Street

Emergency rehabilitation works at Third Street East and Third Street in Walvis Bay, with Nobel Civils appointed as the rail-specialized subcontractor.

  • Main Contractor: OTESA Civil Engineering
  • Supervisor: TransNamib Holdings Limited
  • Scope: Level crossing blocks, track blocks, edge restraints and interlocks
  • Status: Completed with no incidents recorded
2025 • Tsumeb

Namutoni Road Level Crossing

Construction of a level crossing for the Tsumeb Namutoni Road in the Oshikoto Region. The project included railway crossing block installation and related rail works.

  • Client / Consultant: Roads Authority Namibia / Denchi Consulting Engineers
  • Contractor: Nexus Civils Pty Ltd
  • Perway Subcontractor: Nobel Civils
  • Perway Supervision: TransNamib Holdings
2025 • Ohangwena

C45 Omhedi Road Level Crossing

Upgrade of the level crossing on C45 Road in Omhedi, Ohangwena Region, supporting access to the Omhedi / Oukwanyama Palace area.

  • Contractor: Nexus Civils Pty Ltd
  • Railway Works: Nobel Civils
  • Supervisor: TransNamib Holdings
  • Scope: Level crossing blocks and railway safety works
